How it works

From file to proof in four steps.

01
Submit & Hash
Upload your work and we generate a unique SHA-256 fingerprint locally — the file itself is never transmitted or stored.
02
Network Notarization
That hash is broadcast and confirmed across independent nodes, with no single party able to alter or revoke it.
03
Immutable Certificate
You receive a timestamped certificate of registration — viewable, shareable, and verifiable by anyone, anytime.
04
License or Sell
List your asset on the marketplace, set your terms, and collect royalties automatically as it is used or sold.
